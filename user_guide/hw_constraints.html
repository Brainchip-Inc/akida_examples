Hardware constraints¶

While working with CNN2SNN and the Akida simulator, only few limitations are imposed. When mapping a model to the FPGA emulator or to the Akida hardware, more constraints have to be taken into account.

The tables below summarizes the hardware limitations for Akida layers.
